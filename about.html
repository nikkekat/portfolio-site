About

I'm a Product Design student at the University of Minnesota's College of Design, with a minor in UX and a growing focus on experience-driven systems.

My background spans graphic design, print production, customer outreach, and UX wireframing - experiences that have shaped how I approach design holistically. Whether I'm building reusable templates for the University of Minnesota Alumni Association, prototyping event pages, or streamlining print workflows, I'm drawn to work that improves clarity, consistency, and usability.

I care deeply about how design functions in real environments, not just how it looks, but how it works. My time in print production strengthened my attention to detail and quality control. My outreach and sales experience sharpened my communication skills and ability to understand user needs. And my design roles have taught me how brand, structure, and interaction intersect to create meaningful experiences.

I'm especially interested in the intersection of product design and UX, where physical and digital systems meet, and where thoughtful design can make complex processes feel intuitive.
