About Me

I'm a Product Design student at the University of Minnesota's College of Design, with a minor in UX and a growing focus on experience-driven systems.

My background spans graphic design, print production, customer outreach, and UX wireframing — experiences that have shaped how I approach design holistically. I care deeply about how design functions in real environments, not just how it looks, but how it works.

I'm especially interested in the intersection of product design and UX, where physical and digital systems meet, and where thoughtful design can make complex processes feel intuitive.
